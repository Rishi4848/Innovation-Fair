About Us

Vitalis is your health companion that uses accurate and medically approved information to provide personalized diagnoses and prescribe medication that is tailored to your symptoms. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health and well-being, even for those in underdeserved or rural areas.

Features

Symptom Checker

Get a quick and simple diagnosis of your symptoms, all from the comfort of your home.

Medication Recommendations

AI-based prescriptions that consider all your health conditions and symptoms, and provide you with the medicine you need to recover.

Personalized Recommendations

Home remedies and at-home treatment tips for a faster and smoother healing process.

Coming Soon

Health Logs that you can type, record, and save your health information that can use your day to day logs to provide personalized recommendations for a more interactive and better experience and can help you lead a better lifestyle.
